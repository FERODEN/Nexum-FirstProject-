Frequently Asked Questions

How do you approach designing a hotel room?

We start with your brand, target guests, and budget. Then we balance aesthetics, functionality, and durability — because hotel rooms must look great and survive thousands of stays. Our process includes concept development, layout planning, materials selection, and final visualizations before anything is built.

Can you design rooms for different hotel categories (boutique, luxury, business)?

Absolutely. We tailor each design to the hotel’s category and audience. A boutique hotel needs personality and storytelling, a business hotel prioritizes comfort and efficiency, while luxury spaces focus on premium materials and refined details. The goal is always the same: a memorable guest experience that matches your positioning.

Do you work with existing spaces or only new projects?

Both. We design rooms for new hotels as well as renovations of existing properties. When working with existing spaces, we optimize the layout, lighting, and furniture to modernize the room without unnecessary construction costs.
